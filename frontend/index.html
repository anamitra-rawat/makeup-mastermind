About Your Skin

We use these fields to rank by demographic-matched ratings — the average rating from Sephora reviewers who share your skin type and tone (≈1.1 M reviews, real-rating coverage flagged via is_real). Two things to know:
• sensitive → dry proxy. Sensitive-skin reviewer counts are too sparse, so the feature pipeline maps sensitive to the dry rating column.
• 5 user tones, 9 reviewer tones. Sephora reviewers tag in 9 bins (adds fair_light, light_medium, medium_tan, dark); we collapse to 5 here because that's the user-facing space the 800-user training set was sampled from. The in-between bins still live in the product features — they just aren't directly queried by your input.
